# Product brief

Juris is an MVP CRM for one lawyer. It demonstrates how a solo practice can manage clients, matters, deadlines, tasks, documents, and personal Telegram reminders without a complex enterprise interface.

## Agreed MVP scope

- No visible sign-in screen.
- Desktop and mobile layouts.
- Expanded client card and matter pipeline.
- Status changes from the client table.
- Search, filters, and sorting.
- Client editing and confirmed deletion.
- Six to eight realistic demo clients.
- Compact status counters.
- Personal reminders linked to a client and matter.
- Simulated Telegram delivery to the lawyer, not to the client.
- Onboarding tour that explains the main product features.

The project intentionally simulates AI summaries and Telegram delivery. It does not claim to perform legal analysis or send real messages.
